Samvid Music Event

Get ready for the Intercollegiate Music Showdown at Samvid a celebration of musical talent and unity. Showcase your skills, compete with the best, and experience the thrill of live performances. Beyond the competition, connect with fellow music enthusiasts, forming bonds that go beyond the stage. Whether you're a solo sensation, a masterful instrumentalist, or part of a harmonious duet or group, Samvid's Music Event is your platform to express, connect, and leave a lasting musical impression. Join us in this symphony of talent, where notes transform into stories, and melodies resonate with the collective heartbeat of the audience.

CATEGORIES

 1. Solo Singing Competition

 2. Solo Instrumental Competition

 3. Duet Song Competition

 4. Group Song Competition

RULES

 1. Solo Singing: 2 mins, no live music, any language, vocals judged solely.

 2. Solo Instrumental: 3 mins, any genre allowed, participant carries instrument.

 3. Duet Song: 2 mins, carry your instrument, any language, minimum 4 entries required.

 4. Group Song: 3 mins, 4-6 members, carry your instrument, minimum 4 entries required.

ENTRY FEES

 Solo Singing - Rs 150/-

 Solo Instrumental - Rs 150/-

 Duet Song - Rs 300/-

 Group Song - Rs 400/-
