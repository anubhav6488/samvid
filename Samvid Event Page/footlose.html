Footloose Dance Event

Footloose, a spirited celebration of movement and rhythm, invites individuals to liberate their inner dancer and revel in the joy of self-expression. This vibrant event transcends boundaries, welcoming seasoned performers and enthusiastic beginners alike to share the stage. From solo acts that showcase personal flair to dynamic duets that weave storytelling into synchronized moves, and energetic group performances that merge diverse dance styles, Footloose fosters a community where creativity flourishes. With each beat, participants immerse themselves in the magic of dance, creating an electric atmosphere that resonates with passion, unity, and the sheer delight of letting loose on the dance floor.

CATEGORIES

 1. Solo Dance Competition

 2. Duet Dance Competition

 3. Group Dance Competition

RULES

 1. Solo Dance: 3 mins, solo flair, no live music, your style, your accessories.

 2. Duet Dance: 5 mins, sync up, manage audio and props, watch the clock.

 3. Group Dance: 12 mins, 4-30 members, no live music, coordinate audio, props, and time.

ENTRY FEES

 Solo - Rs 250/-

 Duet - Rs 400/-

 Group - Rs 800/-
