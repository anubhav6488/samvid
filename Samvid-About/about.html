About Us

SSTC (shri shankaracharya technical campus) is gearing up to host "Samvid," an electrifying techno-fest that promises to ignite minds and inspire innovation. Samvid is not just an event; it's a platform where creativity meets technology, and ideas transform into reality. With a dynamic blend of competitions, workshops, and guest lectures, participants will immerse themselves in a world of cutting-edge technology and endless possibilities. From hackathons to robotics challenges, Samvid offers something for every tech enthusiast. Get ready to unleash your potential, network with industry experts, and experience the thrill of innovation at SSTC's Samvid techno-fest.

The World Tree

Yggdrasil, the fabled ash tree of Norse mythology, symbolizes the interconnectedness of the cosmos. Towering at the heart of existence, its branches extend across the nine realms, upholding the fabric of reality. Known as Mimir's tree and Lærad, it embodies wisdom and sustenance. Among Germanic tribes, echoes of this cosmic tree resonate in the veneration of Irminsul, revered by Saxons in ancient Saxony, Germany. However, its demise during the Saxon Wars under Charlemagne's conquest marked the end of an era, yet the enduring legend of Yggdrasil continues to inspire awe and reverence, reflecting the enduring power of myth across cultures.
